I founded PDX Industrial Design in 2009, after roles as a Product Designer at Astro Studios and an Innovator in Nike’s Advanced R&D organization. While my portfolio of project examples demonstrates my experience across a wide range of industries and brands, I’ve specialized most deeply in designing technical footwear, soft goods, and wearable accessories.

My core strength is product creation - developing innovative concepts from exploratory sketches to production samples. My network of talented colleagues in Portland allows me to offer clients an extended range of services, such as user research, brand identity, rapid prototyping, mechanical engineering, and sourcing.
